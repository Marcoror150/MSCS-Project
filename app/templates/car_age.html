Help

You are currently reading the help screen for our application. Please read the following sections to help answer any questions you have.

C.A.R.S.: By clicking the C.A.R.S. logo (Crash Analysis Reporting System), you will be redirected to the home page.

Categories: If you click on the Categories drop-down menu, you will be given the option to pick one of three categories. The first option is for analyzing the likelihood of an accident having multiple fatalities given a certain car (make and model). The second option allows you to analyze the the likelihood of a general car accident based on the age of a car. The final option allows you to calculate the likelihood of an accident when given a specific geographic location.
